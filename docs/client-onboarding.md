# Client Onboarding Guide

Welcome to AI Front-Desk! This guide explains what happens after you sign up
and how to get your AI agent answering calls.

---

## What you'll get

- A dedicated phone number your customers call (or forward calls to).
- An AI agent named **Alex** that answers 24/7, qualifies leads, detects
  emergencies, and books appointments directly into your Google Calendar.
- A dashboard where you can view all calls, bookings, and analytics.
- SMS confirmations sent automatically when appointments are booked.

---

## Step 1 — Check your email

After Arup sets up your account, you'll receive two emails:

1. **"Set your password"** — click this link to create your password
   and log into your dashboard.
2. **Setup instructions** — this guide, plus your phone number.

If you don't see either email within 10 minutes, check your spam folder
or contact Arup.

---

## Step 2 — Log into your dashboard

Visit the dashboard URL Arup sent you and log in with the email and
password you set up.

You'll see:
- **Dashboard** — live call count, bookings this week, key metrics.
- **Call Logs** — every call with summary and transcript.
- **Bookings** — all appointments booked by the agent.
- **Analytics** — trends over time (calls per day, booking rate, etc.).
- **Settings** — update your business info, hours, and integrations.

---

## Step 3 — Set up call forwarding

Your AI agent answers calls on the number Arup provisioned for you.
There are two ways to route calls to it:

### Option A: Forward your existing business number
Set up call forwarding on your current business phone to your new
AI Front-Desk number. Your customers keep calling the same number;
calls are silently forwarded to Alex.

Most carriers offer call forwarding in settings, or via a code:
- AT&T: dial `*21*+1XXXXXXXXXX#` (replace with your AI number)
- Verizon: dial `*71+1XXXXXXXXXX`
- VoIP providers: check your admin portal → Call Forwarding

### Option B: Use the new number directly
Give the new number to customers, put it on your website, Google
Business Profile, etc. Any call to that number goes straight to Alex.

---

## Step 4 — Connect your Google Calendar

The agent needs access to your calendar to check availability and book
appointments.

1. Log into your dashboard → **Settings**.
2. Click **"Connect Google Calendar"**.
3. A browser tab opens — sign in with the Google account you use for
   your business calendar and grant the requested permissions.
4. Return to Settings — you'll see a green checkmark when connected.

**Important:** The agent only books appointments during the working hours
you configured. Events already on your calendar block those slots.

---

## What Alex can do

- Greet callers and identify their needs.
- Detect emergencies (burst pipe, gas leak, no heat, sparking wires)
  and transfer the call to your emergency line immediately.
- Verify the caller is in your service area.
- Collect: name, phone number, address, and problem description.
- Check your Google Calendar for available slots.
- Book appointments and send a text confirmation to the caller.
- Answer questions about your services, hours, and pricing.

## FAQ

**Is the AI always on?**
Yes — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including holidays. If you need
to take the number offline temporarily, contact Arup to suspend the agent.

**What if Alex can't help?**
If the caller has a question Alex can't answer, or if they ask to speak
to a human, Alex will offer to take their number and have you call back.
You'll also receive a text with the caller's number if a call ends
without a booking.

**What if I change my hours or services?**
Log into the dashboard → Settings and update your information. Changes
take effect on the next incoming call (current calls are not interrupted).

**How do emergencies work?**
If Alex detects emergency keywords (burst pipe, gas leak, no heat in
winter, sparking wires), it immediately transfers the call to the
emergency phone number you provided — no questions asked.

**Can I see the transcript of a call?**
Yes — every call appears in the Call Logs page with a summary and
full transcript.

**How do I cancel?**
Contact Arup. Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation.
